This project seeks to create awareness about the existence and detection of fake drugs.


Our Mission

Is to prevent the use of counterfeit and substandard medicines through awareness on the existence of fake drugs, dangers of fake drugs and the use of the Mobile Authentication Service to check for fake drugs.

Contact Us

TAKE THE DRUG TEST

Ensure that you are getting genuine medicine.


Send an SMS to 4544 on all the mobile numbers across Africa to find out if the medicine you have bought is genuine.


HOW IT WORKS

  1. Look for the scratch pad on the pack of the medicine.
  2. Scratch the pad to reveal the Serial ID.
  3. SMD to Serial ID to 1393(SMS FREE).
  4. Wait for a NO or OK response.
  5. If the response says NO, kindly send an SMS to the number 3434 or fill our complaint form below.

Social Media Campaigns

Our awareness programs include social media campaigns across all our social media platforms. The campaigns gather and develop resources on fake drugs and share them on our websites and across all our platforms. Publicity

Participation

Ensure you buy your drugs from approved Pharmacy and also verify your drugs with your mobile phone.

Physical Campaigns

Invite us to your community, health centers, schools for both physical and virtual campaigns. The campaigns help equip consumers with the knowledge to help avoid the threats associated with fake drugs and to safeguard their health.

Fake Drug Alert

With the increase in the number of websites selling unapproved and illegal medicines, caution has to be taken before purchasing drugs from online pharmacies. Join us on all our social media platforms as we take you through a series on detecting fake online pharmacies and drug vendors.
